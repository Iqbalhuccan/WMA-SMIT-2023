Importance of High level Languages:

High-level programming languages play a vital role in the world of computer programming,
offering several key advantages over low-level languages. These languages, such as Python, Java, and C++,
are designed to be more user-friendly and closer to human language, making them easier to read, write, and
understand for programmers. The importance of high-level languages lies in their ability to enhance productivity,
promote code reusability, and enable faster development and maintenance of software applications.

Firstly, high-level languages boost productivity by providing a level of abstraction that simplifies complex programming
tasks. With built-in functions, libraries, and advanced data structures, programmers can accomplish more with fewer
lines of code. High-level languages allow developers to focus on problem-solving and logic, rather than getting
caught up in low-level details like memory management or hardware-specific instructions. This increased productivity not only
speeds up the development process but also reduces the likelihood of errors, making programs more reliable and efficient.
